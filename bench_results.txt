### Key Changes in This Commit
1. **ring_wait_idle in synchronize**: replaced `dx12_device_wait_idle` (Signal+WaitForSingleObject) with `dx12_ring_wait_idle` (fence-polling only) — eliminates per-split Signal overhead
2. **GPU timer profiler**: per-dispatch GPU timing via D3D12 timestamp queries (non-intrusive, no I/O during benchmark)
3. **Deferred staging buffer destruction**: prevents RDNA4 compute-queue driver crash (HOW-TO-FIX #10)
4. **DXLA wave alpha_f16**: proper F16 alpha factor for GEMM shader constants

### Notes
- Vulkan pp numbers are still suspiciously high on small models (batching/caching effect); tg numbers are more reliable for comparison
- Vulkan tg outperforms DX12 by 2.8-6.6x across tested models — suggests DXLA wave shader dispatch overhead or suboptimal kernel launch pattern
- GPU TDR (0x887A0006 device removed) on gemma4 E4B Q4_0 tg32 DX12; workaround: smaller batch (-b 256)
- Previous baseline (pre-ring-buffer): gemma4 E4B Q4_0 pp128 = 53.43 t/s on DX12 (ring_wait_idle improved this to 630.76 t/s, ~11.8x)
