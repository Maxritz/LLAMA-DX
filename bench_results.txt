### Key Changes in This Commit
1. **ring_wait_idle in synchronize**: replaced `dx12_device_wait_idle` (Signal+WaitForSingleObject) with `dx12_ring_wait_idle` (fence-polling only) — eliminates per-split Signal overhead
2. **GPU timer profiler**: per-dispatch GPU timing via D3D12 timestamp queries (non-intrusive, no I/O during benchmark)
3. **Deferred staging buffer destruction**: prevents RDNA4 compute-queue driver crash (HOW-TO-FIX #10)
4. **DXLA wave alpha_f16**: proper F16 alpha factor for GEMM shader constants

### Notes
- CX12 pp numbers highly variable due to fence wait in `dx12_graph_compute_end` (stalls per split)
- gemma4 E4B Q4_0 and rocmforge 7B Q8_0 TDR on DX12 for tg tests (GPU timeout, need smaller batch)
- Vulkan numbers appear inflated for pp on some models (possible batching/caching effect); tg numbers are cross-validated
- Previous baseline (pre-ring-buffer): gemma4 E4B Q4_0 pp128 = 53.43 t/s on DX12
